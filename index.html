Jacksonville Announces First Coast's New Independent Film Festival
We are launching LUMINA, a 3-month cinematic residency designed to revitalize Jacksonville's creative economy.

JACKSONVILLE, FL — LUMINA Film Festival, the First Coast's first recurring three-month independent film residency, is set to launch October 30–31, 2026 with its inaugural edition: Reel Fear, a two-day horror and dark fantasy short film showcase timed to Halloween weekend.

The festival will run across three consecutive months under a unified parent brand: Reel Fear (October — horror, thriller, supernatural), Reel Feels (November — drama, documentary, human stories), and Reel Wonder (December — fantasy, animation, winter wonder). Each edition features curated short films and features from independent filmmakers worldwide, filmmaker Q&A panels, awards ceremonies, and VIP receptions.

“Lumina” refers to the light of the projector — the source of all cinema. The festival takes its name from that light because everything it does is in service of it: illuminating stories, amplifying voices, and building a cultural destination on the First Coast.

Why Jacksonville

Jacksonville is the #2 most Halloween-obsessed city in Florida, surpassing Miami and Tampa, and ranks #8 nationally. Research shows that Jacksonville residents are not just willing to drive to seasonal events — they are actively seeking community destinations to anchor their holiday spending. LUMINA opens its inaugural season at exactly this intersection of cultural appetite and creative opportunity.

A three-month cinematic journey in the heart of Jacksonville, starting Halloween 2026. Each month features a new theme, each theme a new audience, and each audience a new reason for the First Coast's creative economy to grow.

Festival at a glance
Editions
Three consecutive months: Oct, Nov, Dec 2026. Each edition is a standalone two-day event with its own theme, palette, and programming.
Programming
30–50 films per edition. Short films (under 20 min), features (20+ min), and student films. All genres welcome within each edition's theme.
Attendance
Maximum 300 attendees per event. Intimate, curated, high-quality. General admission, weekend pass, and VIP tiers available.
Submissions
Via FilmFreeway. Reel Fear opens June 2026. Reel Feels opens August 2026. Reel Wonder opens October 2026.
